Academic Event Designs

❮ ❯


Much of my most recent work was for the Department of Physics & Astronomy. Since COVID-19, many of their scholarships and events that normally would've been advertised primarily as flyers needed to be turned into digital items to be shared via email and web. Then once COVID-19 regulations eased up, those digital ads also needed flyers or slides that could be displayed on TV displays to reach those seeing them in-person.

I only had the text to work with for most of these, so my main goal is always to emphasize the Who, What, Where, When, etc of each project, then emphasize that hierarchy with graphics and color.

Visit the Before & Afters for examples of this in action.
